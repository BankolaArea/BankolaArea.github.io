Welcome to Bankola Area

The Bankola Area, a cornerstone of the local coal mining industry, is dedicated to fostering community well-being and skill development. Through its Safety and Training Departments, it aims to provide a safe and thriving environment for all workers and visitors alike.

Safety Department

The Safety Department at Bankola ensures that all operational practices adhere to the highest safety standards. The department coordinates regular inspections, organizes safety drills, and is always prepared for emergency response scenarios.

Training Department & Group Vocational Training Center

The Training Department, along with the Group Vocational Training Center, offers structured courses aimed at improving the skills of both employees and community members. From technical training to vocational certifications, Bankola's facilities are equipped to enhance productivity and career growth.
